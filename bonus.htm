On a night long anticipated for its tranquility, a piercing scream shattered the

silence—filled with desperation, pleading, and unmistakable terror. It was a cruel

reminder of the man who should never have been freed, a soul unjustly condemned.

For years, he had been chained in a cell that was more tomb than prison, its damp,

crumbling walls alive with mold and rot. The iron shackles had bitten deep, leaving

wounds as raw as the injustice that had kept him there. Now, his face, pale and

scarred by the years of torment, bore the weight of a rage that had festered in the

darkness.

With nothing but vengeance to drive him, he hunts for the one who destroyed his life.

But after so many lost years, what hope remains? And will justice ever outweigh the

fury that now burns within him?
